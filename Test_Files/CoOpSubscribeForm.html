The Natural Enquirer

View the Latest Natural Enquirer Issue!

View PDF here!

Delivered, free of charge, to all active member-owners. It is written by Co-op staff, and features local and store news, product information; features on farmers, suppliers, and producers; announcements; and the upcoming workshop calendar (see below). Additional copies of the newsletter are available at the front registers. Please help yourself!

The Natural Enquirer is edited by Beverly Faxon. If you are interested in advertising, would like to submit a letter to the editor, or have additional questions, she may be contacted at (360) 336-5087, ext. 104.

Advertising rates may be found here (opens as a PDF).

The Co-op Scoop

Get the Scoop! Our weekly e-newsletter is published every Tuesday at 11am. It includes the weekly FRESH DEALS flyer, weekly Produce sales, and biweekly Co+op Deals. It also features cheese, wine, beer & cider, the bulk department, recipes, and upcoming events.

The Co-op Scoop is edited by our Marketing & Outreach Director, Rachael Harper. She may be contacted at community(at)skagitfoodcoop(dot)com.
